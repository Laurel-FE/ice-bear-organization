Our values


We can see nature and our climate deteriorating at an alarming rate, with serious consequences for all species on Earth, including our own.

Our objective is to create a world in which humans coexist with nature. We fight to protect and restore biodiversity, the web that sustains all life on Earth, to decrease humanity's environmental imprint, and to ensure the sustainable use of natural resources for present and future generations.

We value and appreciate diversity in nature as well as among the individuals, partners, and communities with whom we collaborate. We are united by one mission, one brand, and one set of principles shared by numerous cultures and individuals:
  • Courage: We display courage by our acts, we strive for change where it is required, and we encourage individuals and institutions to address the most serious threats to environment and the future of our home planet.

  • Integrity: We live the ideals that we expect others to follow. We conduct ourselves with honesty, responsibility, and openness, and we rely on facts and research to guide us and ensure that we grow and adapt.

  • Respect: We value the perspectives and knowledge of the people and communities we serve, and we seek to ensure their rights to a prosperous future.

  • Collaboration: Through the power of collaborative effort and invention, we produce influence on the scale of the issues we confront.

Our mission & vision



Our mission is to protect polar bears and the sea ice on which they rely. We seek to inspire people to care about the Arctic, the dangers to its future, and its connections to our global climate through media, science, and advocacy.

We envision the long-term survival of polar bears and the unique environment in which they call home.
